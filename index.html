Byrd Boasts about the Beautiful Band
"The Punch Brothers"


Check out their official website at: PunchBrothers.com


The Band is Comprised of

  • Chris Thile - Mandolin and Lead Vocals
  • Gabe Witcher - Violin
  • Noam Pikelny - Banjo
  • Chris Eldrige - Guitar
  • Paul Kowert - Bass
  • All members provide back up vocals!

I saw this band perform live with my Father at one of Dallas' most historical venues: The Majestic theatre.
The lead singer and Mandolin Player also has an amazing show on NPR that features some great music and comedy.
The show is now called "Live From Here" and was formerly "Prarie Home companion." This is a great show for the whole family!

Biography

The Band has an entertaining back story, and went through multiple names before being called "The Punch Brothers."
Check out Wikipedia for the detailed and referenced information. The group started out with just Thile and Witcher as the primary members, but eventually grew into a 5 piece bluegrass fusion band.
They play very virtuostically and really remind the listener of what Nashville Music is all about.
Stories and Love and all the feelings that go with human expression can be felt in their music.
